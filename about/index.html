About me

I’ve been a Software Developer in some professional capacity since 2009. I began my career as a developer in the United States Air Force in 2009, serving for four years and some change. I completed my associates degree while enlisted, and worked in a few different positions after I separated in 2013. I also was employed as a Lead Technical consultant for Robert Half Technology’s Salaried Professional Services division based out of Cleveland for about 2 years (2017-2019). I currently work as the Senior Developer for the National Inventor’s Hall of Fame.

I’ve been an on and off ameteur musician, have dabbled in pixel art and game music composition, and have recently rekindled my love of drawing to a… probably worrying degree (at least if you ask my SO, who lovingly nods their head whenever I show her my work). My end goal is to be able to work on projects for clients outside the realm of having a 9 to 5er, where my knowledge and experience can be put to use for many people, and I have ultimate control over my work life.
